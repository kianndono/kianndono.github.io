Kiann Arcena Peñaredondo

Graduating Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila in October 2025. I specialize in developing AI-powered applications, mobile apps, and web systems, with a strong passion for integrating artificial intelligence into practical, user-centric solutions.

My work includes creating emotional wellness platforms with sentiment analysis and anomaly detection, building beauty salon management systems with intelligent staff-client matching, and developing ride-sharing apps with real-time GPS tracking and payment integration.

As a published researcher at the IEEE ICICyTA 2024, my thesis project EmoShown demonstrates expertise in React Native, Python Flask, and machine learning algorithms such as VADER, Isolation Forest, and collaborative filtering.

I also bring experience in quality assurance, technical support, and operations management, equipping me with strong problem-solving skills and the ability to communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders.

I aim to contribute my skills in dynamic environments where innovation and user experience are prioritized, creating intelligent systems that make a meaningful impact.
